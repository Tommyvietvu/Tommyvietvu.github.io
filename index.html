Anime and Manga

My first childhood memories began with the exploration of Anime and Manga and how it assimilated into my lifestyle as of now. The top anime and manga series that I loved reading and watching were One Piece and Naruto since they were one of the few mainstream anime and manga at the time.

Stepping into the gaming world

Growing up playing video games with my brothers was a joyous event for me because of the impact gaming has brought to my life.For me, playing video games is a source of energy and a way to cope with any stress. I still reminisce the past of playing video games - especially the old online video games such as World of Warcraft, Maplestory, Runescape, and so on. These memories will forever be ingrained in my heart and soul as I continue to progress in my future.
